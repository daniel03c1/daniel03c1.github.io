Hi, I am Daniel Rho

I am a Ph.D. student at the University of North Carolina, working under the supervision of Professor Roni Sengupta. My research focuses on integrating physics into neural rendering techniques and applying neural rendering to medical applications. Also, I have experience in neural fields, audio signal processing and generation, and self-supervised learning.
